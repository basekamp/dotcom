REQUEST FOR PARTICIPATION:
You have been selected to participate in the Connect The Dots exhibition that focuses on artists who curate. We are enlisting your help to contribute your field research and findings to add to a collective knowledge base about an emerging practice that you are a part of. Through this meeting of minds, we hope to co-generate a tangible and noteworthy appendix of strategies for artist curated projects in the forms of an exhibition and printed journal. This will both articulate a current set of specific practices, and serve as a list of possibilities for new work by emerging practitioners.

Your participation in the Connect The Dots exhibition would include:
1. your answers to a questionnaire
2. images, media and/or objects that represent your findings (past exhibitions)
3. permission for the curators to print or fabricate (if necessary) reproductions of your past exhibition(s).
4. Please provide recommendations for several other artist/curators that you are aware of and find to be compelling.

If you are interested and able to participate, please respond by email to: con_nect_the_dots@hotmail.com
