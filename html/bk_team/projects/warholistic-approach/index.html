Campbell's Global Design Mission

• Leverage the knowledge and resources of the Global Design Center in a productive and cost effective manner to develop visual expressions of the brand that will excite, inspire and engage internal and external audiences.

• Develop effective strategic design and brand communication solutions that enable business decisions; influence the target markets purchase decision with compelling customer experiences and positively enhance the position of our brands.

 

Campbell's Global Design Vision

• Serve the Campbell Soup Company as the 'global centre of excellence' for;
translating brand strategy into creative, meaningful, on-target design solutions that communicates the brand promise.

• Maintain the role of corporate brand communication guardian to assure alignment and consistent graphic expression in the deployment of all Campbell Soup Company brands logos and trademarks.

• To be sought after by internal business colleagues and brand partners for brand communication and design expertise; envied by industry peers and competitors for excellence in brand stewardship and consumer communication.

• A leader in design innovation; that is embraced by the organization, influence consumers and establish market segment trends.
