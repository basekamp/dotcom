Residencies

basekamp residency program now open for artists

Studios are adjacent to the Basekamp exhibition space, which focuses on international collaborative projects. The space is run by the Basekamp artist group, who also host weekly potlucks, screenings and reading groups. Artists-in-Residence will have periodic opportunities to collaborate on exhibitions with the group, visiting artists and curators.

 

studio & residency spaces $250 - $350 / month

  • access to large shared workspaces and communal areas
  • wireless internet access
  • wood shop
  • experimental loft area
  • mess hall kitchen
  • shower and laundry facilities
  • 24 hour studio access
  • center-city location
  • month to month (longer available)
  • no deposit required
